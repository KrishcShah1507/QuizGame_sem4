Solutions - General Category


1) What is the capital of France?

Correct Answer: Paris

Paris is not only the capital but also the largest city in France. It is renowned for its art, fashion, cuisine, and culture.

2) Who wrote the play 'Romeo and Juliet'?

Correct Answer: William Shakespeare

William Shakespeare, an English playwright and poet, is widely regarded as one of the greatest writers in the English language.

3) What is the chemical symbol for gold?

Correct Answer: Au

Gold has been highly valued throughout history for its rarity and beauty. Its chemical symbol 'Au' is derived from the Latin word 'aurum'.

4) Which luxury brand logo has an animal in it?

Correct Answer: Ferrari

Ferrari's logo features a prancing horse, which is a symbol of power, speed, and luxury.

5) How much is a baker's dozen?

Correct Answer: 13

A baker's dozen consists of 13 items instead of the usual 12. This practice originated from bakers adding an extra item to avoid penalties for selling underweight goods.

6) Which of the following plays is referred to as a 'Tragedy'?

Correct Answer: Hamlet

'Hamlet' is one of William Shakespeare's most famous tragedies, exploring themes of revenge, betrayal, and existentialism.

7) Which year was the 'iPhone' released?

Correct Answer: 2007

The first iPhone was released by Apple Inc. in 2007, revolutionizing the smartphone industry with its touchscreen interface and innovative features.

8) Who was the first living organism in space from Earth?

Correct Answer: A Dog

Laika, a Soviet space dog, became the first living organism from Earth to orbit the Earth aboard Sputnik 2 in 1957. Unfortunately, she did not survive the journey.

9) Which is the largest organ in the human anatomy?

Correct Answer: Skin

The skin is the largest organ of the human body, serving as a protective barrier against pathogens, regulating temperature, and facilitating sensory perception.

10) What is the most used social media app?

Correct Answer: YouTube

YouTube is the most popular video-sharing platform and the second most visited website in the world. It allows users to upload, view, rate, share, and comment on videos.
